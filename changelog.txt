Civilization VI game balancing mod (DL version)
Based on Civ6, Dec 2020 update (New Frontier Pass)

[Version v0.1.0]

[Gameplay]
Remove game cost escalation (except purchase plot).

fixed cost of districts.

dam 200.

All projects cost 75 production except

[Yields]
change terrain & feature yields.

调整了专家产出，增加对应类型的2点伟人点产出。
